About Me

Hey there, i'm Eric. I've always been the creative type deep down, but never knew it myself. In my final year of high-school I completed a major project, designing a house from the bottom up and I loved the process. But coming out of high-school, I knew that I didn't want to design houses for a living; though I still wanted to be creative. I ended up taking a combined degree studying Business and Science in Information Technology not really knowing where I wanted to go yet career-wise. I decided to major in Accounting (Business) and Programming (IT).

Throughout high-school I dabbled with HTML and CSS for fun and even made things with Visual Basic in class, but never fully immersed myself into the world of programming. Two years into my degree I realised that accounting was not for me, but I pushed through and ended up finishing my combined degree. I found that I was having fun and was a lot more interested in the world of tech and creating things with code than I was with accounting. So, after looking into it I decided to make it my goal to be a web developer, while continuing to crush all my uni classes!

So far, I'm enjoying every second of the learning process. I love the problem solving aspect of it, and being able to see a working finished product that I created is really fulfilling.
